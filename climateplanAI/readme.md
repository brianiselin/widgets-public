# ClimatePlanAI

ClimatePlanAI applies advanced artificial intelligence and machine learning to help cities and organizations implement actionable climate adaptation and resilience strategies. By transforming climate data into meaningful insights, ClimatePlanAI supports real-time decision-making and sustainable urban planning.

## Mission

To empower decision-makers with AI-driven climate resilience solutions that turn ambitious climate targets into funded, executable projects.

## Features

- AI-powered climate risk assessment and forecasting  
- Interactive dashboards and custom reporting  
- Facilitates prioritization and funding of climate initiatives  
- Supports policy and operational climate action planning
