# Iselin Group

Iselin Group is a strategic consultancy dedicated to empowering organizations to thrive in an evolving global landscape. We specialize in crafting strategies that address complex challenges in business, human rights, international relations, and innovation.

## Our Mission

To provide insightful, ethical, and impactful consultancy services that drive sustainable success and positive global impact.

## Our Activities

- Strategic consulting across business, human rights, and international affairs  
- Management of diverse subsidiaries including human rights consulting, policy institutes, and creative design studios  
- Facilitation of international forums and think tanks such as the Europe-Taiwan Forum and Institute  
- Brokerage of strategic products and services with a focus on emerging technologies like satellite communications and drone surveillance  
- Development of digital products including web components and interactive data visualization widgets
