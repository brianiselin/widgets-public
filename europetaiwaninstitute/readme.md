# Europe-Taiwan Institute (ETI)

The Europe-Taiwan Institute is a dedicated platform fostering stronger engagement and cooperation between European countries and Taiwan. ETI conducts policy research, promotes academic and cultural exchanges, and facilitates dialogue on key issues including trade, democracy, innovation, and regional security.

## Mission

To deepen EU-Taiwan relations through inclusive research, policy analysis, and collaborative initiatives that support shared democratic values and sustainable growth in the Indo-Pacific region.

## Activities

- Conducts research on EU-Taiwan trade and political relations  
- Organizes seminars, workshops, and dialogues involving policymakers, academics, and business leaders  
- Promotes cross-regional innovation and technology cooperation  
- Monitors geopolitical developments impacting Taiwan and Europe  
- Supports educational and cultural exchanges
