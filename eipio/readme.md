# European Institute of Professional Intelligence Officers (EIPIO)

EIPIO is Europe's leading professional institute dedicated to advancing the standards, ethics, and practice of intelligence and security across the continent. We provide education, research, and a collaborative network for intelligence professionals spanning multiple sectors.

## Mission

To promote excellence and professionalism in intelligence through cutting-edge education, rigorous research, and vibrant community engagement.

## Activities

- Offering specialized professional training and certification programs  
- Conducting intelligence research and analysis on emerging threats and trends  
- Facilitating inter-sectoral networking and knowledge exchange  
- Advocating for ethical practices and standards in intelligence operations  
- Supporting collaboration among intelligence practitioners, academics, and policymakers
